--- layout: post title: 4-dimensional cohomogeneity one gradient Ricci solitons speaker: Patrick Donovan profile-pic: blank.jpg speaker-url: speaker-inst: - UNSW category: geom-pde-reading-seminar-season06 youtube-id: c5EQl_jkyVI abstract: Gradient Ricci solitons are generalisations of Einstein manifolds and are important in the study of Ricci flow. Gradient Ricci solitons with strong symmetry are well studied in dimension 3 and below. We construct new large families of asymptotically conical 4-dimensional expanding cohomogeneity one gradient Ricci solitons. We also investigate the uniqueness of compact cohomogeneity one gradient Ricci solitons. ---
